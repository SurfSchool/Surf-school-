Hello There!

Varkala Surf School: Your Gateway to Aquatic Adventures

Welcome to Varkala Surf School, the premier destination for water sports enthusiasts since 2005. Immerse yourself in the thrill of the ocean as you ride the waves, glide on a stand-up paddleboard, and embark on exciting kayaking excursions.

Our highly skilled instructors are dedicated to providing exceptional surf lessons tailored to your skill level, whether you're a beginner eager to catch your first wave or an experienced surfer looking to refine your technique. Equipped with top-of-the-line gear, we ensure your safety and maximize your stoke.

In addition to surfing, we offer stand-up paddleboarding (SUP) lessons, allowing you to explore tranquil waters while engaging your core muscles. Experience the serenity of gliding across the water and discover hidden coves and marine life in a peaceful setting.

For those seeking a more adventurous experience, our kayaking lessons are designed to take you on thrilling water journeys. Navigate through winding waterways, marvel at stunning coastal landscapes, and encounter the wonders of nature up close.

Safety is our utmost priority. Our certified instructors maintain a low student-to-instructor ratio, ensuring personalized attention and guidance. With regularly inspected equipment and a welcoming atmosphere, we create a safe and enjoyable environment for all.

Join us at Varkala Surf School and unlock the magic of the ocean. Whether you're a local or a visitor, let us be your guide to unforgettable aquatic adventures. Book your lesson today and get ready to make a splash!
